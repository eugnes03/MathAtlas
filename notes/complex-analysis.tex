\begin{definition}[Holomorphic Function]
  \label{def:holfunction}

  A holomorphic function is a complex-valued function of one or more complex variables that is complex-differentiable around the neighbourhood of every point in 
  its domain $U$.

  There are a few equivalent definitions of this such as the complex interpretation of the classical calculus definition of a derivative, 
  the Cauchy Riemann equation, and also the implication that they are equivalent to analytic functions (seen later).
\end{definition}
