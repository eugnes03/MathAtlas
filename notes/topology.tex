\begin{definition}[Metric Spaces]
  \label{def:metricspaces}
  A \emph{metric space} is a set $X$ together with a function 
  $$ \operatorname{dist}: X \times X \to \R $$ 
  called a \emph{metric} such that the following laws are satisfied: 

  \begin{enumerate}
    \item (Positivity) $\operatorname{dist}(x,y) \ge 0$ with equality $x = y$
    \item (Symmetric) $\operatorname{dist}(x,y) = \operatorname{dist}(y,x)$
    \item (Triangle Inequality) $\operatorname{dist}(x,z) \le \operatorname{dist}(x,y) + \operatorname{dist}(y,z)$ 
  \end{enumerate}
\end{definition}
