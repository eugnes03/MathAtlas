Definition
Category Theory · category.tex
Let $$ F,G : \mathcal{C} \to \mathcal{D} $$ be functors (Definition~Functor). A natural transformation $$ \eta : F \Rightarrow G $$ consists of morphisms $$ \eta_X : F(X) \to G(X) $$ for every object $X$ of $\mathcal{C}$ such that for every morphism $$ f : X \to Y $$ the following diagram commutes: $$ \begin{tikzcd} F(X) \arrow[r,"\eta_X"] \arrow[d,"F(f)"'] & G(X) \arrow[d,"G(f)"] \\ F(Y) \arrow[r,"\eta_Y"] & G(Y) \end{tikzcd} $$
