Problem

Character Table of $S_3$

Representation Theory · example.tex
Compute the complete character table of the symmetric group $S_3$. Verify the column orthogonality relations: \[ \sum_{\chi \in \hat{G}} \chi(g)\,\overline{\chi(h)} \;=\; |C_G(g)| \cdot \delta_{[g],[h]}. \]
