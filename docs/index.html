DefinitionGroup Representation
Representation Theory · example.tex
\label{def:representation} A representation of a group $G$ on a vector space $V$ over a field $k$ is a group homomorphism \[ \rho : G \to GL(V). \] The dimension $\dim_k V$ is called the degree of the representation.
DefinitionSubrepresentation
Representation Theory · example.tex
\label{def:subrepresentation} A subspace $W \subseteq V$ is a subrepresentation (or $G$-invariant subspace) if $\rho(g)(W) \subseteq W$ for all $g \in G$. This notion relies on the setup of \ref{def:representation}.
