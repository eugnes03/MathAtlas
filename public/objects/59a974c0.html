Regular Representation

Representation Theory · example.tex
The regular representation of $G$ is the action of $G$ on the group algebra $k[G]$ by left multiplication: \[ \rho(g) \cdot \sum_{h \in G} a_h \, h \;=\; \sum_{h \in G} a_h \, (gh). \] Over an algebraically closed field of characteristic zero, every irreducible representation appears in $k[G]$ with multiplicity equal to its degree.
